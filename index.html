About

More on me

Hello! I am a final year undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering in the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India. I primarily work on Machine Learning and its applications in Robotics. I also dabble in Control Systems and Motion Planning every now and then.

I like to explore the applications of the latest advancements in the rapidly growing field of AI and ML to robotics. Over the course of my undergraduate studies, I have worked on a variety of projects based on topics ranging on from Generative AI applications for robotics and reinforcement learning to motion planning, guidance theory and control systems.

When I am off the academic grind, I enjoy travelling and listening to music. I also enjoy go-karting and you can find me on the track every now and then. From time to time, I find myself down historical and geopolitical rabbitholes, and I'm always up for a good chat on these topics.
